Theme

Problem statements will be based on following tech domains.

Agriculture & Rural Development:- We envision vibrant, inclusive and sustainable rural economies, where people live free from poverty and hunger. Our mission is to transform rural economies and food systems by making them more inclusive, productive, resilient and sustainable.
Dial 100 & Dial 108:- One of the largest and most ambitious projects ever undertaken by the police force,is Dial 100 and dial 108 project which is designed to provide integrated emergency services across the entire state, including rural areas, on a 24 x 7 basis.
Smart Vehicle:-A Smart Vehicle system is an electrical device that enhances the automobile safety and practice of designing vehicles, construction, equipment, and regulation to minimise the occurrence and consequences of traffic collisions.
Robotics & Drone:-Drones and robotic technology can be used with the goal of improving the quality and productivity of the power grid maintenance, which will ultimately support reliability and cost-effectiveness.
Clean & Green Technology :-The goal of clean and green technology is to protect the environment, repair damage done to the environment in the past, and conserve the Earth's natural resources.
Cyber Security& Blockchain:-Cyber security is the practice of defending computers, servers, devices, networksand data from malicious attacks along with Blockchain, a Distributed Ledger Technology (DLT), which is focused on creating trust in an untrusting ecosystem, making it a potentially strong cybersecurity technology.
Aiml :- Aiml which aims to develop a strong foundation by using the principles and technologies that consist of many facets of AI including logic, knowledge representation, probabilistic models, and machine learning.
IoT :- IoT is a system of interrelated computing devices, mechanical and digital machines, objects, animals or people that are provided with (UIDs) and the ability to transfer data over a network without requiring human-to-human.
Miscellaneous:- Miscellaneous consisting of a mixture of various things that are not necessarily connected with each other
